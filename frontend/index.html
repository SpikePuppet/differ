A considered revision of the Stripe adapter

Modified

The adapter, in its previous incarnation, held too many opinions. Retry logic lived beside serialization; serialization lived beside the very network calls it was meant to describe. In this article the author performs a quiet dissection — severing the intent construction from the transport, and returning the module to its plainer voice.
